Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

W.H. Academy ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our website at whacademy692.github.io/whacademy-/.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a feedback form or enrollment form through our website, the following information may be collected:

  • Your name
  • Your city / location
  • Your school or education board
  • Your class and subject preferences
  • Your feedback or comments
  • Your contact details (if provided in forms)

This data is collected via Google Forms and stored in Google Sheets, which are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display student feedback/testimonials on our website (with your permission)
  • To contact you regarding enrollment and tutoring sessions
  • To improve our services based on feedback

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Our website does not use cookies or tracking scripts. We do not use Google Analytics or any other analytics tool at this time. External services (Google Fonts, Font Awesome CDN) may set their own cookies per their privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Forms / Sheets: Feedback and enrollment data
  • Google Fonts: Typography
  • Font Awesome (cdnjs): Icons
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting

5. Data Security

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages with HTTPS enforced. All external links include security attributes. User-submitted content displayed on our site is sanitized before display.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for students of all ages including minors (Class 6-8). We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information from children without parental consent. Enrollment forms are intended to be completed with parental awareness.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your submitted feedback or enrollment data by contacting us at whacademy692@gmail.com. We will process such requests within 7 business days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
